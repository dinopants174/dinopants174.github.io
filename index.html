ABOUT ME

I am hoping to someday study or work abroad.

Currently, I reside in Needham, Massachusetts, where I am enrolled as a freshman at the Olin College of Engineering. While I do not declare a major for a few semesters, I do intend to pursue Electrical and Computer Engineering, which I discovered I enjoyed while at Olin.
